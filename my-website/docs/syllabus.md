# Syllabus: Physical AI & Robotics

This syllabus outlines the topics covered in this comprehensive documentation on Physical AI and Robotics. It is designed for a deep dive into the theoretical foundations and practical applications of embodied intelligence.

## Modules

1.  **Foundations of AI for Robotics**:
    *   Introduction to Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Reinforcement Learning in the context of robotics.
    *   Sensor Fusion and Perception for physical agents.

2.  **Robotics Fundamentals**:
    *   Kinematics and Dynamics of robotic systems.
    *   Control theory and motion planning.
    *   Robot operating systems (ROS) and simulation environments.

3.  **Humanoid Robotics**:
    *   Design and actuation principles of humanoid robots.
    *   Bipedal locomotion and balance control.
    *   Human-robot interaction and safety considerations.

4.  **Embodied AI Systems**:
    *   Learning in embodied agents.
    *   Cognition and reasoning in physical environments.
    *   Development platforms and case studies of embodied AI.

5.  **Ethical and Societal Implications**:
    *   Responsible development of Physical AI and robotics.
    *   Impact on labor, privacy, and warfare.
    *   Regulatory frameworks and public perception.

This syllabus provides a structured path through the material, ensuring a thorough understanding of Physical AI and Robotics.
